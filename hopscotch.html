Creative expansion to Pose Hopscotch

  • Designed an improved version of the popular kids-game 'Hopscotch'.
  • Hopscotch is a fun and simple game, but it lacks variation in the gameplay and an agency for strategy.
  • Redesigned the game to incorporate an engaging gameplay and make it fun for both kids and adults alike.
  • In this version, all players are split into teams of two. Every round, both players in the team begin simultaneously at opposing ends.
  • At every hop, both players must co-ordinate their jumps and land in the same pose at the same time.
  • Through playtests, it was found that 'Posing' is a simple-yet-creatively-satisfying mechanic.
  • During playtests, It was interesting to see players trying to communicate effectively. The game encouraged social interactions.
