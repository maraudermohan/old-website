Factory Fiasco - 'Temple Run' from the side view.

  • This personal project is about designing a side-scrolling platform game for mobile [iOS].
  • Before designing the game, I researched on mobile games to understand the market and the demographics better.
  • Based on that research, the game was designed for one of my favorite genres - fast-paced, action platformer.
  • Documented the mechanics and aesthetics are detailed along with the key takeaways of my research.
  • Level design document holds a sample level, aimed for a short-and-fast gameplay with an engaging interest curve.
  • Temple Run, Ninjump and many other iOS games have been an inspiration for my design.
