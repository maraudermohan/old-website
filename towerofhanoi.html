Work Summary

Tower of Hanoi - strategy board game

  • Designed this strategy based board game as part of a team building activity.
  • To add some depth to the gameplay, we designed the game around the principles of 'Tower of Hanoi' puzzle.
  • 'Tower of Hanoi' - 3 circular disks are stacked on top of each other to form a conical shape.
  • The disks can be unstacked and moved one disk at a time. But the constraint is - only a smaller disk can be placed over a bigger disk. Not other way around.
  • Designed the game keeping a toy as the core element of the game - stackable pyramids.
  • To create a nice balance between chance and strategy, we introduced dice mechanic for movement.
  • As a team, we came to an agreement that the demographics for our game - Adults.
