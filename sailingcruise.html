Sailing Cruise - Building Virtual Worlds #2

  • Assignment project for 'Building Virtual Worlds' class during Masters course.
  • The assignment was to create a world - simple and intuitive enough for a naive guest without any help from the developers.
  • Team : Cintia higashi (Programmer) , Daniel Lin (2D artist), Kaiyang Zhang (Sound designer) and Myself (3D artist).
  • Implemented effective 'Indirect control' in our world, by using visual and auditory elements that aided the guest in his quest.
  • Designed the dolphin as the main guiding-force of the guest, always trying to lead the sailboat to the destination.
  • Used the Head-Mounted-display and its pointers as the input interface for the guest to control the sailboat.
  • Interestingly, we used a boat-prop to hold the HMD pointers in place. This played to our advantage, as the naive guest felt that the prop made the controls easier and intuitive.
  • Designed the animated sharks, penguins and whale to add visual pleasure to the world, keeping the guest engaged.
