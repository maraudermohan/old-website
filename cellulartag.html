'Cell'ular Tag - game expansion

  • A personal game design project - variant of the 'Tag' game that was designed to interest adults as well as kids.
  • ‘Tag’ is a fast-paced, fun game involving chasing and tagging players. The game is one of children’s all-time favorites because of its simplicity and ease to play.
  • On analysis of the original game, I figured that it lacked strategy.
  • Also adults lose interest soon because they grow tired of just running around.
  • For an adult, a game would be fun when it has a mix of strategy and skill.
  • This variant of Tag gives the players a choice between just running hard versus running less with a bit of strategy.
