2 level designs - a) Egyptian ruins & b) Industrial room

  • A personal level-design project for the first person shooters like Portal.
  • Along with Portal, I took Tomb raider and Prince of Persia games as references.
  • Tools :   Autodesk Maya & Adobe After effects.
  • Attempted to balance the game's puzzle solving nature with the perils that the level holds.
  • Added elements to enhance the fun gameplay of 'arcade style' reflexive movements with heavy combat.
  • The videos are presented in both the FPS view and 3rd person.
  • I took references from ancient Egyptian places like Temple of Philae and the great 'Temple of Abu Simbel' while designing the second level.
