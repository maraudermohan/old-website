Our project at Google I/O (June 27th, 2012)

  • Worked as Lead designer to build an engaging, 3D WebGL game for the Chrome browser.
  • Project was a collaboration between our Carnegie Mellon student team and Electronic Arts, Redwood Shores.
  • Successfully designed the core features that combines tower defense with Player-vs-player combat.
  • Main responsibilities included Systems design, Technical design and UI/UX design.
  • Designed the Weapons system, A.I system, Upgrade system and monetization models.
  • Designed a mobile version, which served as companion app to the original gameplay.
  • Also worked with the artists on designing the visual style as well as UI of the game.
  • Our game was taken over by EA officials, completed and demonstrated at the Google I/O conference in June, 2012.
