Level Design - Action-Adventure game


  • In this level design I have attempted to balance the fun gameplay of 'arcade style' reflexive movements with heavy combat.
  • Action-adventure is my favorite genre in video games. I am also a huge fan of ancient Egypt. So I was really inspired to design a basic level that mixes the best of both.
  • I took references from ancient Egyptian places like Temple of Philae and the great 'Temple of Abu Simbel' while designing this level.

Animated execution of the level


  • A personal level-design project for an action-adventure game.
  • Tools :   Autodesk Maya, Adobe After effects and Adobe Illustrator
  • In this video, the level is presented in a third person, over-the-shoulder camera.
  • The red cubes represent the enemies and the patrol-paths they take.
  • The blue cube represents the player avatar.
  • The blue particle that moves through the level represents the likely path the player avatar will take. It can be compared to the 'locator' in Deadspace 2 or the magical compass in Prince of Persia(2008).
