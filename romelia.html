Romelia - Building Virtual Worlds #4

  • Assignment project for 'Building Virtual Worlds' class during Masters course.
  • The assignment was to create a virtual world with the theme of movie-like grandeur.
  • Team : Jing Li, Elwin Lee, Chirag Raman, Marc Mundy, Mohit Sadhu and Myself.
  • Worked on a epic story, while having meaningful interactions for the player.
  • Used Audience Interaction using color detection technology as our interface.
  • The audience were given colored shields and swords of different colors.
  • Choosing the right prop and lifting it up to the video camera, triggered the attack and defense interactions in the game.
