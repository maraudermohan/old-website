Work Summary

Three Musketeers meet 'Chess'

  • Designed this game in the design workshop at Game Developers Conference, 2012
  • Aim : To understand the design principles of an existing game and then expand it with additional features.
  • At GDC, four of us came together to form a team for this exercise. The good rapport that we had within the team was reflected in the quality of the game that we created.
  • The existing game given to us was Three Musketeers 1969 , a two player abstract strategy board game.
  • Designed a variant of the game with additional features like a new character with special moves and a definite goal.
  • Playtesting the game with external testers enabled us to tackle some crucial design flaws and balance the game.
  • At the end, we created a game playable by 2 - 4 players.
