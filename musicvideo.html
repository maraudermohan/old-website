Visual Story #3



3 Weeks
(Nov 2011)



Music Video


  • Team : Romain Deciron, Lusha Zhang, Anthony Hildebrand, Kevin Primm and Myself.
  • Assignment : Develop, plan and shoot a music video based on the assigned theme, object and color.
  • The challenge was to tell a story that does not follow that of the lyrics nor the original video of the band
    The theme, object and color assigned to us were Substance addiction, a scarf and Toxic orange respectively.
  • We chose to go with Vast's 'Pretty when you cry' and we came up with a crazy story of a guy who was addicted to 'oranges' beyond imagination.
  • This was done for Educational purposes only, as part of the curriculum at Entertainment Technology Center, Carnegie Mellon University.
