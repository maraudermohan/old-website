Draw Anything - a multi-device pictionary

  • Designed the game as part of team building assignment
  • The assignment was to brainstorm and design a fun social game for multiple iOS devices connected via Bluetooth.
  • The target demographic was North American casual mobile gamers.
  • We designed a local-multiplayer version of Pictionary, taking 'Draw Something' as our inspiration and benchmark.
  • We included four different modes of Pictionary in our design, namely - a) Quick draw; b) Team battle; c) Jigsaw pictionary; d) Telephone pictionary.
  • The key challenge of this project was designing a fun gameplay that takes full advantage of the multi-device setup - devices connected locally.
  • Detailed the mechanics and gameplay with the mock-up screenshots in the design document.
