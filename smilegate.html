  • Designed a fun social game for multiple iOS devices, connected by Bluetooth.
  • Project was a collaboration between our Carnegie Mellon student team and SmileGate, Korea.
  • Concept of our game : a competitive multiplayer car-racing game with 'Temple-Run' style controls.
  • Setup for our game was - a central iPad serving as the host and the game screen with multiple iPhones as controllers.
  • Designed the game to meet the project requirements from our clients - a) Local multiplayer game using Bluetooth b) Appealing to American casual mobile-gamers c) Simple, Gesture-based controls and d) Car racing game.
  • Designed and incorporated gameplay features that encouraged social interactions between the players.
  • Successfully balanced the competitive vs. co-operative elements in our game, thus increasing the overall fun quotient.
