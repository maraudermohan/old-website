Ryusei - Building Virtual Worlds #3

  • Assignment project for 'Building Virtual Worlds' class during Masters course.
  • The assignment was to build an interactive world which had a strong storyline.
  • Team : Liu Xiyuan (Lead Programmer) , Weiwei Huo (Producer) , Elwin Lee (2D Artist) , Anthony Hildebrand (Sound Designer ) and Myself (3D Artist).
  • The assignment was to build an interactive world which had a strong storyline.
  • Designed an engaging story where the player's interactions aided to the plot with an unexpected ending.
  • Concentrated on maintaining a good interest curve with appropriate changes in the gameplay elements and the overall story-arc.
  • For the interface, we used Wii-mote hidden in a craftfully made foam-sword.
  • Used Organic Motion's Mo-cap studio in our department and Autodesk Motionbuilder to capture a fellow student's sword actions and incorporated them into the game.
